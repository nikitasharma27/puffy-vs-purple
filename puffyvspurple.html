Puffy Lux Pros and Cons

The Puffy Lux has won Best Mattress two years in a row now and is the company’s best-selling model for a reason. Made of gel-infused blended memory foam, it has four distinct layers that provide cool, cozy sleep to all sleepers. Puffy Lux: Pros
  • The Puffy Lux has two distinct layers dedicated to cooling - Cooling Cloud™, and Climate Comfort™ - so that you’re assured breezy sleep all night long.
  • The added Plush Dual Cloud layer, along with a Firm Core Support layer, helps provide 99% better responsiveness and 4 times the support of a standard mattress.
  • If you’re looking for something a little more structured, the Hybrid option has a layer of contour-adapt coils for added edge support and stability.
  • The stain-resistant cloud cover allows sleep to be stress-free, especially if you have children or pets who are accident-prone, with no-fuss cleanup.
  • The grip base cover sits perfectly on any style of bed frame and won't slip and slide.
Puffy Lux: Cons
  • Heavier sleepers will want to choose the Puffy Lux Hybrid for better support.
  • No setup services with delivery.
With its hybrid option, medium-firm feel, and unique cooling qualities, it’s easy to see how the Puffy Lux is a versatile choice for anyone in the market looking to invest in a high-quality mattress.
Best For Sleepers looking for cooling comfort that still provides support. Couples who share their bed and need advanced motion transfer.
Reconsider If You have a smaller budget. You’re a heavier sleeper on the search for an all-foam mattress.

Purple Hybrid Pros and Cons

The Purple Hybrid mattress contains the company’s patented gel grid and combines it with support coils for firmer, supportive sleep. Unlike the Puffy, the mattress does not have a foam-only option. Purple Hybrid: Pros
  • Thanks to the structure of Purple’s gel grid, air is able to circulate more easily even through the coils of your mattress, keeping your mattress as temperature-neutral as possible.
  • The responsive support coils present in the Purple Hybrid ensure motion-responsiveness, ensuring you’re well-supported no matter what position you find yourself sleeping in.
  • The StretchMax Cover is a polyester-Lycra blend that keeps the mattress breathable while staying soft to the touch.
Purple Hybrid: Cons
  • Unlike the Puffy Lux, the Purple Hybrid does not have any customization options, which means you’ll need to be sure a hybrid mattress is what you’re looking for.
  • Purple’s gel grid has a fine dust layer applied over it to prevent any sticking, something more sensitive sleepers should be aware of.
  • The Purple Hybrid does not come with any sort of removable cover, which limits care to basic spot-clean, making it less family-friendly.
The Purple Hybrid is a really great option for anyone on the hunt for a hybrid mattress, with an innovative design that has set the company apart from its competitors.
Best For Sleepers who want temperature-neutral sleep. Sleepers who don’t require more than a foam mattress.
Reconsider If You want flexibility in your shopping options. You have children or pets that are likely to make accidents or spills on top of your mattress.

So, Which Should You Buy?

The Puffy Lux and Purple Hybrid both have qualities that would make them an excellent choice for side and back sleepers looking for a premium mattress to sleep better.
We would recommend the Puffy Lux between the two, thanks to the superior warranty, better value, and ease-of-clean. The Puffy Lux is a cozy, family-friendly option that provides cooling sleep to a wide range of sleepers.
With the Puffy Lux Hybrid, you also have the option for added edge support and added structure to your sleep. The Purple Hybrid does not come with a foam-only option and does not have a removable cover, making it a challenge to keep clean and maintain.
The Puffy Lux also comes with 2 free Puffy Pillows, as well as a fixed $350 discount, whereas the Purple Hybrid’s discount varies depending on the size of the bed you’re opting for.
In a nutshell: to invest in cozy sleep while feeling good about your purchase, your best bet is a Puffy.
